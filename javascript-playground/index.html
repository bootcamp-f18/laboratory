What is Pokemon Go?

It's a GPS-based augmented reality game. Users move in the real world and interact with wild Pokemon. To capture a Pokemon, users "throw" Pokeballs at them. If caught, the Pokemon is added to the user's Pokedex.

Pokemon Go unified the planet and introduced world peace in 2016.
